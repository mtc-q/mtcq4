FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is mtcq really free?

Yes — free use, excellent service is the whole idea. No paid tiers, no card required, no features held hostage. Create your account, claim your name, and everything on this page is yours.

What exactly is a "tree"?

Your tree is your public page: one URL that holds all the links you want to share. Instead of juggling ten links across bios and captions, you share one — your tree — and people pick where to go.

How do the stats work?

Every time someone opens your page we count a view, and every time they tap a link we count a click for that specific link. Your dashboard shows totals, a per-link breakdown, and highlights your most popular link.

Can I customize how my page looks?

Yes — choose from designed wallpaper presets or set any custom color, add a display name and bio, and attach PNG images to individual links. Changes appear on your live page the moment you save.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. Everything happens in a simple dashboard: type, click, save. If you can fill out a form, you can run a tree.

Is my account information private?

Your email and account data are private to you. Only your public tree — the display name, bio, and links you choose to publish — is visible to visitors.
